About us

E-access Bulletin is produced by Headstar, an innovative new media company specialising in electronic publishing, online events and research relating to the information society. For more information visit www.headstar.com.

The Bulletin's editor is Dan Jellinek, Headstar founder and an experienced national journalist. The Deputy editor is Derek Parkinson, senior reporter is Mel Poluck and news reporter is Julie Hill. Kevin Carey, director of the technology charity HumanITy and an author and broadcaster on technology access issues, is the editorial adviser to the project. For contact details, see 'contact us'.

The main sponsor of the Bulletin is the Royal National Institute for the Blind, is one of the United Kingdom's largest charities, helping all blind and partially sighted people in the UK - almost a million people. It exists to challenge the disabling effects of sight loss by providing information and practical services and products.

For more information see the RNIB web site, email Helpline@rnib.org.uk or telephone 0845 766 99 99.
