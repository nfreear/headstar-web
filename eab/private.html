Our email list privacy promise

Headstar Ltd and the RNIB promise that the email addresses on the circulation list for E-access Bulletin will be held in complete confidence and will never be published or sold on to a third party.

The list will be used solely for circulating the monthly bulletin, and for occasional operational notes (for example if there is a delay in publication). It will not be used for separate commercial mailings.

Any subscriber may unsubscribe themselves from the list at any time by emailing eab-subs@headstar.com with 'unsubscribe eab' as your message header. Once you are unsubscribed, all information held on you will be removed from our circulation database.

Headstar Ltd and E-Access Bulletin are registered data controllers under the Data Protection Act 1998.
